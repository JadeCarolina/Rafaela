Davi: Welcome! Today we are going to explore one of the most fascinating places in Brazil: the Lençóis Maranhenses. Get ready to learn about the history, geography, climate, and beauties of this natural paradise. The Lençóis Maranhenses, located in the state of Maranhão, form a national park with more than 1,500 km². This vast territory hosts an incredible meeting of white sand dunes and freshwater lagoons that appear during the rainy season, creating a unique landscape in the world." Geographically, the Lençóis Maranhenses are situated on a coastal plain, where sand brought by the winds from the interior accumulates, forming the dunes. The region's climate is tropical, with two well-defined seasons: the rainy season from January to June, when the lagoons form, and the dry season from July to December, when many of them evaporate. This natural dynamic creates an ever-changing environment where each visit can reveal a different landscape." The dunes are shaped by the winds, and the lagoons, fed by the rains, are a true miracle of nature. The region is inhabited by traditional communities that live in harmony with the environment." Tourism has grown in the region, but it is essential to preserve it. Sustainable tourism initiatives help protect this natural gem, and local guides play a crucial role." The Lençóis Maranhenses are a dream landscape, marked by their unique geography and dynamic climate. A place that deserves to be visited and revisited. Thank you for joining us on this journey."

Rafaela: Where to find these culinary varieties in Lençóis Maranhenses? What are their prices? Is it worth it?


if your accommodation is in Barreirinhas, you'll find a wide variety of restaurants with very attractive prices for such a touristy city. Common meals can cost less than twenty reais, while more upscale and elaborate meals range from sixty to eighty reais for two people. It's worth noting the proximity of the Preguiça River, which results in a competition among restaurants to attract tourists with varied menus and locations near the river. The waterfront of Barreirinhas has a wide range of restaurants, among the most popular are Bambu and Restaurante A Canoa, which are located by the river and offer the most elegant and refined menus. Restaurante Jacaré, on the other hand, offers exotic dishes at more affordable prices. Another restaurant a bit further from the waterfront, but with a deck for bathing in the Preguiça River, is Restaurante Terral, which offers fresh fried fish and even hammocks in the water for relaxation. These are some of the varieties you can find in Lençóis Maranhenses.



Roberta: Best time to visit. December is the perfect time to enjoy still-full lagoons in Santo Amaro and save on affordable tours. Between January and April, it's the rainy season and the lagoons are filling up. May and June have full lagoons and unstable weather. From June to August, the lagoons are full and the weather is perfect. September and October see the lagoons emptying but still with perfect weather. From October to December, the lagoons are mostly empty, but the weather remains perfect.
